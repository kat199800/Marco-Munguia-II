Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Marco and creative financing in San Diego.

What is creative financing in real estate?

Creative financing refers to non-traditional loan structures — like seller financing, subject-to, hard money, and DSCR loans — that allow buyers to purchase property without conventional bank approval. Marco specializes in these strategies for San Diego and Utah buyers.

Does Marco speak Spanish?

Yes — Marco is fully bilingual and provides complete real estate services in both English and Spanish. He proudly serves San Diego's diverse Latino and Spanish-speaking communities. Hablo Español.

What areas does Marco serve?

Marco serves all of San Diego County, California (including La Jolla, Chula Vista, National City, Mission Valley, El Cajon, and more) and the state of Utah, including Salt Lake City and surrounding areas.

Can I buy a home if the bank denied me?

Yes — this is exactly what Marco specializes in. Through seller financing, subject-to deals, private money, and other creative structures, many buyers who were denied by banks have closed with Marco's help.

What is a DSCR loan?

A DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) loan qualifies you based on the rental income a property generates — not your personal income or W-2. This is ideal for investors and the self-employed who can't document traditional income.
